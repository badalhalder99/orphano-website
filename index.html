How To Buy

Click here to view our video tutorial, alternatively follow the text tutorial below to find out how to buy.

Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet

Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in form of a browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network-list. (Click here for a step-by-step tutorial)

Buy and send BNB to MetaMask

Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a "0x"


Head on to PancakeSwap and swap for Orfano

Click here to head on over to PancakeSwap or use this address to select Orfano. Set the slippage tolerance to 3% (on error you may change it up to 4%, if that doesn't fix the error, change the Orfano output amount to end with a "0" as the last digit [i.e. 12345.123 => 12340])

View Orfano and HODL

Swap BNB for Orfano. Now you need to add Orfano to your MetaMask (Tutorial here) to view your Orfano. Lastly HODL!

Frequently Asked Questions

Since there's a lot of questions that might come up exploring a new token. Here's a couple of the most frequently asked ones (already answered).

How much goes to charity?

A: 100% of the money raised for charity goes to actual charitable purposes. It does not get used anywhere else.

What does locked liquidity mean?

A: liquidity is locked and 25% is locked for future exchanges. Locked means the devs have no access to the liquidity meaning they can't sell it.

What charities do we donate to?

A: At first we will be donating to the smaller charities that the community decides on, soon after our goal is to launch orfano foundation and do the boots on the ground ourselves.
